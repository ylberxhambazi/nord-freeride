Cat Skiing at Sharr Mountains

Nord Freeride Cat-Skiing

Nord Freeride is a Catskiing/Snowcat adventure covering 40+ Ski/Snowboarding fresh powder slopes (7km+ each slope) within 7+ different Sharr Mountains with elevating peaks starting from 2200m to 2658m, Grand Rudoka (2658m) is the highest peak in Kosovo.

Almost all of our slopes (90%+) are faced on the North side (See 3D Google Pics below) therefore the quality of the snow is unique. We receive numerous major storms, which grants us reliable snow from Mid December to late April. The cat-skiing terrain at Nord Freeride is gigantic and covers more than 150 square kilometers, with over 40+ named descends at such a wide terrain, freeride and backcountry skiing options are unlimited here, every day new and untouched slope, so get ready to tighten up your sleeves and hit all our fresh powder rides.

Also, our food is special, everything is organic (meet, dairy, fruits, vegetables, vine, alcohol etc) from 2000m+ heights.

Nord Freeride lodge (1550m) is only 1.5 hours drive from Prishtina International (Kosovo) airport, Snowcat will wait for you at the doorsteps for every morning from 1550m so you can go shredding slopes from peaks as high as 2658m.

Come and join this unforgettable journey!
