Nord Freeride Cat-Skiing

Freeride skiing in Europe, Off-piste skiing in Europe, backcountry skiing in Europe, you name it!¬†

All of our runs are faced North (very few at north east & north west) and all our runs start from 2400m to 2600m (since our Snowcats can climb at these hights) with average 2500m to 1700m descents, therefore at these hights we always receive numerous major storms which grants us reliable snow from late December to late April along with the best quality of snow.¬†The cat-skiing terrains at Nord Freeride is gigantic and covers daily 4000m+ of vertical descents including our tree runs for foggy days. One thing is for sure, you will never run through the same line for the whole week.¬†

We are based at two seperate Sharr Mountains:
